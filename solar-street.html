A street Lighting System is a lighting system for the illumination of streets, roads, squares and common public areas. It uses the sun's light to work. It is the complete set of Solar Panel, Lithium Battery, Light, Charge Controller & Poles.

Solar street light is used to light Streets, Highways, Parks, Gardens, and To Secure Boundary Walls of a Factory, School, and College Campus. Some remote areas such as hill areas of Himachal Pradesh and Assam, College Campus, National Highway Roads, Parking Areas, Public Places in Villages such as Temples, Rivers, etc. do not get appropriate electricity as they are connected with the grid. Solar street light is a boon for such places. It offers a perfect solution for the illumination of streets & squares and crossroads located in areas that are not connected to the power grid.
