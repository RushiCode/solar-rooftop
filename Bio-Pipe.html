Bio-Pipe Technology

  • What is Biopipe ?
  • Biopipe Advantages
  • A Solution for any volume of treatment

Biopipe is a revolutionary biological sewage treatment system that treats water to irrigation quality without producing sludge.

Backed by years of research and development, the Biopipe packaged system offers a unique value proposition for consumers, companies, and governments seeking reliable, easy to maintain, and sustainable water management solutions. Biopipe consists of modules of biopipes resulting in a scalable system perfect for tight and small locations.

  • No Sludge Production

  • Odorless

  • Virtually Silent

  • Low Footprint

  • Low Energy Consumption

  • Flexible and Modular Design

  • Low O&M Cost

  • Automated/Remotely Monitored

Biopipe can be installed wherever domestic wastewater (black and grey water) is produced / generated regardless of size and capacity.

Because of its modular design, the Biopipe system can be scaled up or down to accommodate different capacities.

Small System/10-50 m3/day

For Office buildings, Restaurants, Schools, Low-rise condominiums

Medium System/50-100 m3/day

For Hotels, Condominiums, Prisons, Hospitals, Shopping Malls

Large System/ 100-1000 m3/day

For Housing complexes, Labor Camps, Industrial Parks
