Supply of E-Vehicle

BVG India Limited is India’s largest Integrated Service Company which started with 8 members in 1997 to around family of 80,000+ as on today.

Our mission is to look to the future and surge ahead with our mission of taking humanity ahead!

Apart form core business activities, BVG group operates India’s largest Emergency Medical Service fleet with around 1,500 Ambulances., Asia’s largest Police Support Service fleet with around 1,000 vehicles, PMPML’s 100 buses along with the driver and maintenance solution.

We have the exclusive association with Mahindra & Mahindra Limited for procurement of e-vehicles, the letter confirming the same is attached:

Overview of Potential Customers for Supply of E-Vehicles

  • Central as well as State Government Departments.

  • Municipal Corporations .

  • Judicial as well as Quasi Judicial Departments.

  • Income Tax Departments.

  • Private as well as Public Sector Companies.

  • Other private sector where BVG has a business association.

Our Solution Includes:

We propose to provide the E-Car facility in following three options:-

  • Supply of E-Cars Only: Under this option, we only propose to provide the e-car on pre-decided monthly charges or on per km basis.

  • Supply of E-Cars with trained drivers: Under this option, we propose to provide the e-car along with the trained driver on pre-decided monthly charges or on per km basis.

  • Supply of E-Cars, Driver, Charing Solution and Maintenance: Under this option, we propose to provide the e-car along with the trained driver, we also provide the charging solution along with the maintenance of the e-car on pre-decided monthly charges or on per km basis.
