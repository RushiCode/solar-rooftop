Bvg clean energy

BVG Clean Energy Ltd. is an entity formed by BVG India Ltd. for clean energy technology and new initiatives. BVG Clean Energy Ltd. is working on Solar Energy, Nanotechnology products, Biopipe, Energy audiT, E-vehicles and more such sectors which are in line with clean energy and making the future better for living. BVG Clean Energy Ltd. is a leading organization in providing clean energy solutions to industrial, residential and other commercial sector consumers in India.

We are one of the pioneers in developing various alternate energy solutions based on solar resources. Our products are designed to meet various applications and energy demands of the customers. We provide cost effective end to end solutions in the areas of solar PV installations, solar water heating, solar pumps, solar lighting, open access power contracting, energy audits, consultancy services, supply, system integration, energy tracking and monitoring.

Want to Know About Us?

BVG Group, in a leading player in renewable energy segment and is developing Power Capacity of 240 MW across the globe. BVG also has strong footprints in Engineering, Procurement and Construction of Renewable Energy and Electrical Projects.


The group has strong footprints in ASEAN & Africa region. The Group had a mandate to develop 10 MW Ground Based Project in Osmanabad for which the land and power evacuation including transmission line for 10 MW is commissioned. On the same land, the Group has developed and Commissioned 2 MW land based project in Osmanabad, Maharashtra. We are also executing 200 MW of Renewable Energy Project in Cambodia and Developing 30 MW Solar PV IPP project in Africa.
